History

Here's a brief history for Swiftwater Rafting Co.:
Early Days Swiftwater Rafting Co. was founded in 2023 by Lawani Covenant and Paul, Daniel and Shadrach Lawani. Covenant's passion for rafting and sharing the thrill of the river with others led him to start small, guiding friends and family on local rivers. Growth and Expansion As word of Jack's expertise and infectious enthusiasm spread, Swiftwater Rafting Co. grew rapidly. By the early 2024, the company had expanded to offer guided trips on multiple rivers across the western United States. A new generation of guides, trained by Jack himself, joined the team, bringing fresh energy and expertise. Safety and Innovation In the early 2000s, Swiftwater Rafting Co. pioneered new safety protocols and equipment innovations, setting industry standards. The company's commitment to safety and customer satisfaction earned them a reputation as a leader in the rafting industry.
Present Day
Today, Swiftwater Rafting Co. operates on over a dozen rivers, offering a range of trips from gentle floats to adrenaline-pumping whitewater adventures. With a team of experienced guides, top-notch equipment, and a passion for sharing the joy of rafting, Swiftwater Rafting Co. continues to thrill and inspire river enthusiasts of all ages.
